## Simple Interest Calculator

A calculator that calculates simple interest given principal, annual rate of interest and time period in years.

```# Simple Interest Calculator

This project calculates simple interest based on the principal amount, rate of interest, and time.

## Formula

Simple Interest = (P × R × T) / 100

Where:
- P is the principal amount
- R is the annual rate of interest
- T is the time in years

## Example

If P = 1000, R = 5%, and T = 2 years:

Simple Interest = (1000 × 5 × 2) / 100 = 100

## Description

This calculator helps users quickly compute simple interest by taking input values for principal, rate, and time.

Input:
   p, principal amount
   t, time period in years
   r, annual rate of interest
Output
   simple interest = p*t*r
```
